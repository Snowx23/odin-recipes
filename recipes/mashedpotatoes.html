Mashed Potatoes

Description

This is an American dish that includes homestyle buttery mashed potatoes, pork chops,

sweet corn, gravy and cheesy chopped beans. It tastes like an absolute party!

Ingredients

  • Packet of ready to make homemade buttery mashed potatoes.
  • Pork chops.
  • Can of chopped beans.
  • Packet of sweet corn.
  • Packet of gravy.
  • American cheddar cheese slices.

Steps

  1. Mix the homemade mashed potatoes with water according to the instructions on the package.
  2. Cook the pork chops for 10 minutes in a pan and then proceed to cook them in an oven for 30-45 minutes on 350 fahrenheit.
  3. Add the beans to a pot with 2 slices of cheddar cheese and cook until everything is mixed and the cheese has melted.
  4. Warm the gravy in a microwave until it's hot.
  5. As soon as the pork chops are done cooking, add everything onto a plate and pour the gravy atop.
  6. Enjoy!
